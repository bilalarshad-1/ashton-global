Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Ashton Global Services LLC (“Company,” “We,” “Our,” or “Us”) operates ashtonglobalservices.com and provides digital solutions. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose your personal information when you access our Website or use our digital services (“Service”). By using our Service, you consent to the practices described herein. If you do not agree, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to deliver and improve our digital services. This includes:

  • Personal & Contact Information:

    Your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, company name, and job title.

  • Service-Specific Information:

    Data you provide when registering, submitting project inquiries, service requests, or other communications related to our digital projects.

  • Technical & Usage Data:

    Information automatically collected from your device (e.g., IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, timestamps) to help us analyze trends and enhance user experience.

  • Cookies & Tracking Technologies:

    Cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies used to remember preferences and track usage.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tools to:

  • Enhance your browsing experience and remember your preferences.
  • Analyze website traffic and usage trends.
  • Enable targeted marketing communications (only with your explicit consent).

You can manage or disable cookies via your browser settings; however, disabling them may impact the functionality of our Service.

4. Data Privacy & Opt-In Consent

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All OPT-IN requests include text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with third parties.

5. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for various purposes, including:

  • Service Provision & Personalization:

    Processing orders, delivering digital solutions, managing your account, and tailoring our services to your needs.

  • Communication & Customer Support:

    Responding to inquiries, sending project and account updates, billing notices, and other important communications.

  • Service Improvement:

    Analyzing usage data to enhance our Website and develop new features.

  • Marketing & Promotional Communications:

    • Consent-Based: We send promotional emails, SMS, or other messages only if you have provided explicit consent (e.g., via opt‑in checkboxes).
    • Opt‑Out Options: You can withdraw your consent at any time by using unsubscribe links or replying “STOP” to SMS messages..
    • No Third‑Party Marketing: We do not sell or share your information for third-party marketing without your explicit permission.

  • Legal & Regulatory Compliance:

    To meet contractual obligations, comply with legal requirements, and protect our rights and those of our users.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We share your personal information only with trusted parties under strict confidentiality:

  • Service Providers & Vendors:

    Third-party contractors (e.g., payment processors, hosting providers, analytics vendors) who help deliver our Service.

  • Business Partners & Affiliates:

    Organizations collaborating with us to support our digital solutions.

  • Legal & Regulatory Authorities:

    When required by law, regulation, or court order, or to protect the safety and rights of our users.

  • Business Transfers:

    In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset transfer, ensuring continued protection of your data.

Unless you have expressly consented, we do not sell or rent your personal information.

7. Data Security

We implement industry‑standard security measures (e.g., encryption, access controls, regular security assessments) to safeguard your personal information. While we strive for maximum security, no method is completely foolproof; you acknowledge the inherent risks of online data transmission.

8. Data Retention and International Transfers

  • Data Retention:

    We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law. Once no longer needed, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

  • International Transfers:

    Your data may be stored or processed on servers outside your home country (e.g., in the U.S. or Pakistan). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under 18 (or the applicable age of majority), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately for prompt deletion.

10. Compliance with U.S. and Pakistani Laws

We adhere to all applicable U.S. federal and state laws (including the CCPA and FTC guidelines) and Pakistani data protection regulations. Our practices are regularly reviewed and updated to comply with evolving legal requirements in both regions.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a new “Last Updated” date. Your continued use of our Service after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
